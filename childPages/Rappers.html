Drake

Aubrey Drake Graham was born in Toronto, Ontario, on October 24, 1986. Drake was popped in the year of 2011, after the show "Degrassi" had jumpstarted him. This hype had caught the attention of various mainstream artists, such as Lil Wayne, who signed the young man to his record label "Young Money Entertainment".\ After this record deal he had go on to make songs that are recognized by those around the world, his genre in rap has stretched from rap singing, to mellow rap. Drake's most viewed song on "Youtube" is "Life Is Good ft Future", with a whopping 2 billion views!

Kanye

Kanye Omari West was born in Atlanta, Georgia, on June 8, 1977. Kanye's upcoming into the rap world was accompanied with his best friend "Jay-Z". "Roc-A-Fella records" had signed him in 2002, which is around the time he dropped out of college to purse his career of rap. Kanye's style is outlandish and unpredictable, always shocking you. Kanye has not only boomed the rap culrure in terms of music, but has added a whole new clothing style to it, with the release of his famous sneaker, the "Yeezy".

Kendrick Lamar

Kendrick Lamar Duckworth, born in Compton, California on June 17, 1987. The childhood of Kendrick was tough, residing in a area of high crime. This pain reflects the lyrics of his songs and is the prime reason people appreicate his music, since they relate to the never ending loop of pain. The signing to the infamous rapper Dr. Dre's record "Aftermath Entertainment" had led to him exhibiting his lyrics to the mass of the world, with the album "m.A.A.d City". This album had struck the rap culture, marked number 2 on the 200 billboards. The flow of his music is verstaile, giving you a array of songs to choose to satisfy your mood.
