Rap History

The history of rap is eventful, the rap we listen to today is constructed from the ages and obstacles rap culture went through.

The founding of Rap

Rap as a genre formed at block parties in New York City in the young 1970s, when DJs started separating the percussion breaks of funk, soul, and disco songs and expanding them. MCs entrusted with familiarizing the DJs and preserving energized crowd would talk between songs, joke and generally interact with the audience.

The Art of Rap

The true meaning of rap is to release the suffocation of your thoughts and feelings. For some it was the urge to present the world with the cirmcumstances they endured at a early age and for others it was taken as a way of mindfulness, clearing their mind of negativity. Rap was furthermore used at a grand scale, showing the corruption of the governemnt towards the African-American race, how they mistreat and marginilize them.

The Evolution of Rap

Rap has envolved, scattering in various paths, each branching off to a other to this day. The initial style of rap was slow-paced, mellow, loose. Leaping to the current times rap arrives in quick and slow speeds. The clarity of the lyrics are fuzzy in few songs as well, which is called "Mumble Rap". This shift in the culture is mainly due to the globalization of the world, frequently bringing in new aspects to throw into the songs. Overall the history of rap has changed the lifestyle of people, especially young teens who are exposed to this music the most.
